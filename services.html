Focus on Growing Your Business. We'll Handle the Inspections.

Property inspections are one of the most important responsibilities in property management—but they're also one of the most time-consuming.

Traveling to properties, conducting detailed inspections, taking photographs, testing fixtures, compiling reports, and following up with clients can take hours out of your team's day.

At PropEye, we become an extension of your business by taking care of the entire inspection process professionally and independently.

This allows your property managers to focus on what they do best—serving clients, managing properties, securing new mandates, and growing your portfolio.

Comprehensive Property Inspections

Our inspections are designed to provide a complete picture of the property's condition. Unlike a basic checklist inspection, we conduct a thorough visual and operational assessment.

Our inspections include:

✔️ Interior & exterior inspections
✔️ Move-in and move-out inspections
✔️ Routine property inspections
✔️ Holiday home inspections
✔️ Vacant property inspections
✔️ Testing accessible light switches and light fittings
✔️ Testing accessible plug points
✔️ Checking accessible built-in fixtures and appliances (where applicable)
✔️ Operating taps and flushing toilets
✔️ Inspecting windows, doors and locks
✔️ Recording water and electricity meter readings
✔️ Identifying visible leaks, damp, mould and maintenance concerns
✔️ Comprehensive photographic documentation
✔️ Professional digital inspection reports
